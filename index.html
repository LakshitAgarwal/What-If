Join us for a thought-provoking and intellectually stimulating event: "What If?"
The event is designed while keeping in mind the inquisitive ones who are eager to explore the possibility of imaginative yet impactful scenarios, engage in meaningful discussions, and put their critical thinking skills to the test. (The maximum team size is limited to two people.)
And stand a chance to win prizes worth 10k!!

Participation guidelines:

• You have to choose one of the problem statements provided below, and submit your creative solutions, arguments, statistical data graphs, whatever your heart desires in a document form.
• After the submission round from 1st to 6th November the best amongst you will get to present at the live event on 7th nov with a firey QnA round.
• Stand a chance to present your work to the Dean and other esteemed faculty, build connections and win exciting prices from our sponsors!!
